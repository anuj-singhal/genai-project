ACME CORPORATION - EMPLOYEE POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

1. WORK HOURS AND ATTENDANCE
- Standard work hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday
- Flexible working arrangements available with manager approval
- Remote work permitted up to 3 days per week for eligible positions
- Core hours (10:00 AM - 3:00 PM) require all team members to be available
- Overtime must be pre-approved by department managers

2. LEAVE POLICIES
- Annual leave: 21 days per year for full-time employees
- Sick leave: 10 days per year, medical certificate required after 3 consecutive days
- Parental leave: 12 weeks paid for primary caregivers, 4 weeks for secondary
- Bereavement leave: 5 days for immediate family members
- Personal days: 3 days per year, no questions asked

3. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
- Bi-annual reviews conducted in June and December
- 360-degree feedback system implemented for all positions
- Performance ratings: Exceptional, Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement
- Merit increases based on performance ratings and market adjustments
- Promotion cycles occur quarterly with manager nominations

4. TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
- Annual training budget of $2,500 per employee
- Mandatory compliance training completed within 30 days of hire
- Leadership development program for high-potential employees
- Tuition reimbursement up to $10,000 per year for relevant degrees
- Conference attendance approved on case-by-case basis

5. CODE OF CONDUCT
- Professional attire required in client-facing roles
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- Confidentiality agreements mandatory for all employees
- Conflict of interest must be disclosed to HR immediately
- Social media policy restricts discussion of proprietary information
